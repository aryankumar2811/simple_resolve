# SimpleResolve — 2-Minute Demo Script

> Approx. 300 words narration · 8 scenes · target pace: ~150 wpm

---

## SCENE 1 — Landing Page Hero (0:00–0:12)

**Screen:** Landing page loads. Hero animates in.

**Narration:**
"Every AML system today does the same thing — freezes the entire account and waits for a human to review it. SimpleResolve is a four-layer AI pipeline that catches real threats while keeping legitimate customers transacting."

---

## SCENE 2 — Legacy Comparison + Four Layers (0:12–0:28)

**Screen:** Scroll to comparison table. Pause briefly on each row. Then scroll to the four layer cards.

**Narration:**
"Instead of static rules, we use continuous behavioral profiling. Instead of a binary freeze, an LLM picks the minimum restriction. Instead of manual investigation, an 8-node agent pipeline runs in under four minutes. The human analyst makes one decision: file or dismiss."

---

## SCENE 3 — Scenario Viewer (0:28–0:48)

**Screen:** Scroll to "See the pipeline output." Click through the three scenario tabs: Structuring, Crypto Layering, Mule Account. Pause on each layer's output.

**Narration:**
"Here's what the pipeline actually produces. Take Bob — three deposits just under ten thousand dollars from new counterparties. Layer 1 scores him at 0.87 risk and flags structuring. Layer 2 restricts e-transfers and crypto but keeps bill payments and payroll active. Layer 3 drafts the STR narrative with mapped FINTRAC indicators — ready for human sign-off."

---

## SCENE 4 — Dashboard Clean State + Run Simulation (0:48–1:05)

**Screen:** Navigate to Dashboard. Show the clean state — all clients say "Awaiting analysis." Click "Run Simulation."

**Narration:**
"This is the operations dashboard. Ten seeded clients, no analysis yet. One click runs the full pipeline across all of them. In production this triggers automatically on every transaction event."

---

## SCENE 5 — Simulation Running (1:05–1:22)

**Screen:** Show inline simulation panel with progress bar, step log scrolling, client chips appearing. Click "Expand" to show full two-panel modal briefly.

**Narration:**
"Each client flows through all four layers in sequence. You can see behavioral analysis, the LLM reasoning through restriction levels, and the investigation orchestrator working in real time. Alice auto-resolves in seconds. Bob gets flagged and restricted."

---

## SCENE 6 — Dashboard Updated + Client Detail (1:22–1:40)

**Screen:** Close modal. Dashboard shows updated risk levels, scores, archetypes. Click into Bob Kamara (L3 Restricted).

**Narration:**
"The dashboard now shows every client's risk level, behavioral archetype, and score. Bob is Level 3 — restricted. His profile shows exactly what's blocked: crypto and new-counterparty e-transfers. Suspicious transactions are highlighted in red with the specific reason. Everything the client can still do — bill payments, known contacts — stays open."

---

## SCENE 7 — Investigation Detail (1:40–1:55)

**Screen:** Click "View Full Investigation." Show FINTRAC form sections 1-5 briefly. Switch to Agent Steps tab — expand one step. Switch to Evidence tab.

**Narration:**
"The investigation page is a complete FINTRAC STR form — pre-filled by the AI. Every flagged transaction is tagged with indicator codes. The Agent Steps tab shows the full pipeline trace — each step is numbered, labeled by layer, and expandable. The analyst reviews the package and makes one call."

---

## SCENE 8 — Human Decision + Close (1:55–2:00)

**Screen:** Show the sticky footer with "Submit to FINTRAC" and "Raise to Supervisor" buttons. Click "Raise to Supervisor" — show the modal with analyst list and priority selector.

**Narration:**
"File it, dismiss it, or raise it to a supervisor. That's the only human step. Everything else is handled."

---

## END CARD (2:00)

**Screen:** Fade to SimpleResolve logo + tagline.

> **Stop freezing your customers. Start resolving risk.**
